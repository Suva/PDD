Six months later...

What went wrong?

Cutting corners

  • No more automated tests
  • Bad QA
  • Lots of hacks
  • Code has become unmaintainable

Lost communication

  • Nobody knows what exactly is in the system
  • Architecture is not synced

People

  • Demotivation
  • Panic immunity
  • Leaving
  • Burnout

There is no such thing as 110%

Everything over 100% is borrowed

Technical debt

  • Is monitored
  • Is repaid regularly

Work borrowed on overtime comes at expensive interest

  • People get tired and are less effective
  • They make more mistakes that have consequences
